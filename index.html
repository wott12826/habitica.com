Powerful Features

Everything you need to turn your life into an epic RPG adventure

Habits

Flexible habit tracker with positive and negative reinforcement. Build good habits and break bad ones with customizable rewards and penalties.

Dailies

Recurring tasks with customizable schedules. Set daily, weekly, or custom intervals with checklists and streak tracking.

To-Dos

One-time tasks that disappear when completed. Accumulate experience and gold over time as you tackle your projects.

Rewards

Custom rewards for completing tasks. Treat yourself with in-game items or real-world rewards you set for yourself.

Gamification

Experience points, health, levels, gold, and mana. Watch your character grow stronger as you complete real-world achievements.

Social Features

Join parties, guilds, and challenges. Complete quests with friends and motivate each other to achieve your goals.
